Width padding (`  8` in the 3-wide `%3d` field) and `>>>` passthrough both confirmed on real output.
- **Key finding — why warp doesn't move the badge:** the badge samples `MHz_eff = cycleCount / wall` at the instant the banner *streams* (~cyc 200M, `build_dsrdb`), which is BEFORE the warp-collapsible phases (delay/timeout spins and post-prompt idle). At that point `warpCycles ≈ 0`, `K ≈ 1`, so `MHz_eff ≈ MHz_real ≈ raw throughput`. No warp variant can change a number measured before the warps fire. `show config` run promptly reads the same ~8 for the same reason (no idle dwell yet). To display a warp-inflated figure you must sample LATER — `show config` after dwelling at `P00>>>` (IF the prompt-wait routes through the `krn$_idle`@0x7bad0 loop `IDLEWARP` hooks — unconfirmed), or just read the exit `WARP-ACCOUNTING:` line. The early banner sample is inherently near raw throughput; changing the displayed value is a sample-point decision, not a warp knob.
